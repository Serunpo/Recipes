Curry Risotto Omurice



Curry Risotto Omurice is a dish made by Sōma Yukihira during the 43rd Annual Tōtsuki Autumn Election Preliminaries.

What you need:

  • Mango, Apple, Banana
  • Honey, Soy sauce, Oyster sauce
  • Curry Powder
  • Garlic, Onion, Chillie
  • Cumin, Coriander, Salt, Pepper
  • Rice
  • Beef Stock
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs, Cream, Milk

What you need to do:

  1. Begin chopping Mange, Apple, and banana to a fine diced and set aside in a bowl.
  2. For the glaze. In a pot, pour some honey, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and some water and reducing that over medium heat until it becomes a glaze.
  3. Before cooking the chutney, finely chop a garlic and some chillies. Cook with oil in a pot and add some spices like coriander, cumin. After cooking, add the chopped fruits and add a good amount of sugar and will make it thicken.
  4. After reducing the glaze, set it aside.
  5. While coming up to a simmer, add in chopped parsley, grated ginger and lemongrass.
  6. To start the Risotto, add oil, chopped onions, ground beef, and curry powder. After it is cooked, season it with salt and pepper.
  7. Pour the rice (Preferably arborio rice) in a pot with the beef with curry powder. Let it cook for a bit then add in the Mango chutney and add in Beef stock bit by bit till it becomes creamy. Season to taste.
  8. Add some grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. In a seperate bowl, add eggs and a little bit of cream and milk.
  10. Pour the eggs in a non stick frying pan in over medium high heat with little bit of melted butter until the curds just begin to set a little bit then stop.
  11. Add the Risotto in the middle of the egg blanket and genlty fold the egg blanket around the Risotto. Slowly invert it to a plate, shape it if needed and drizzle some glaze into the the omurice.
